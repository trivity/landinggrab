Frequently asked questions

Does this work with end-to-end encrypted chats?
Chrome blocks extensions from accessing end-to-end encrypted Messenger chats (the `/e2ee/` URLs). Inbox Extractor works on regular Messenger conversations. Most business pages, older chats, and many personal chats still use the standard non-encrypted mode.
Do you store my messages or link data?
No. Everything happens locally in your browser. Links go directly from Messenger to your Google Sheet or downloads folder. We never see your data.
Do I need a Google account?
Only if you want to export directly to Google Sheets. CSV, XLSX, and clipboard exports work with no account needed.
What if it stops working after a Messenger update?
Updates are included for life. If Facebook changes their page structure, we'll push a fix via the Chrome Web Store automatically.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. If it doesn't work for you within 7 days of purchase, email us for a full refund.
Does it work on Safari or Firefox?
Not yet — Chrome and Chrome-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera) only for now.
